Who We are?

Kavita Cration, established in 2021 in Lohaghat Uttarakhand, with a vision to take Greenery to your doorsteps across the state of uttarakhand and has transformed itself into an innovative, efficient and an extremely reliable GREENERY provider to a large number of dream home and corporates workplaces in Uttarakhand.

Our understanding of home and Workspace Plantscaping is unmatched in the industry; and our products & solutions blend in the workspace as an integral part of the Interior and exterior Designs.

We strongly believe that Greenery and Nature should be a cohesive unit of the home and workplace. It is not only gives a fresh & lively outlook to your home and workplace; but also showers loads of health benefits to the home and workforce; which ultimately boosts their productivity & performance.

In a very short span of time; we have been fortunate to get associated with top clients of the uttarakhand like Rekha, Chinu, Tinku and Bhuwan in lohaghat, just to name of few among our 100+ prestigious client in Uttarakhand. We have not only provided our Greenery Products & Solutions to them; but we have been maintaining their workspace greenery intact for last one year now.

We are looking forward to get associated with valued partners like Customers, Employees, Suppliers and Channel Partners who believe in our vision of creating world class greenery in workplaces with tremendous passion, imagination and love towards Nature.

Our Story

In the winter of 2021, a couple in lohaghat, Bechalor of education and a computer science graduates shared a common vision to create a company based in India, with a niche in greenery and indoor and outdoor decoration. a month later – "Kabbu Gamla" was founded in their home in lohaghat, with a few small projects and 1 director and 1 CEO on board.

To begin with, Kabbu Gamla executed several small projects including super handicraft, traditional and their trademark kabbu gamla.
